## 2. Add Your First Channel

Start tracking YouTube channels to discover viral content.

**Steps:**
1. Click "Channels" in the sidebar
2. Click "Add Channel" button (top right)
3. Paste a YouTube channel URL (e.g., `https://www.youtube.com/@MrBeast`)
   - You can find this by visiting any YouTube channel and copying the URL
4. Click "Add Channel"
5. Wait 5-10 seconds for the initial sync
6. See the channel appear with basic stats!

**What happens next?**
- ReatorAI fetches the channel details (name, description, subscriber count)
- Videos are synced in the background (this can take 1-2 minutes for large channels)
- You'll receive a notification when sync is complete

**Free Plan Limits:**
- Up to 5 channels
- 100 videos per channel (most recent)
- Daily sync for new videos

## 3. Find Outlier Videos

Outliers are videos performing significantly better than the channel's average - these are the ones you want to study!

**Steps:**
1. Click "Videos" in the sidebar
2. Click "Outliers Only" filter toggle (top of page)
3. Browse videos that are performing 2x+ better than average
4. Sort by:
   - View count (highest first)
   - Published date (newest first)
   - Outlier score (biggest outliers first)

**Understanding Outlier Detection:**
- ReatorAI calculates each channel's average performance
- Videos with 2x+ the average views are marked as outliers
- The outlier badge shows the performance multiplier (e.g., "3.2x")

**Pro Tips:**
- Recent outliers (last 7-30 days) are most relevant for trending topics
- Higher outlier scores (4x+) indicate exceptional performance
- Look for patterns across multiple outliers from the same creator

## 4. Analyze a Video

Get AI-powered insights into why a video went viral.

**Steps:**
1. Find an outlier video you want to study
2. Click on the video card to open details
3. Click "Analyze Video" button
4. Wait 10-20 seconds for AI analysis
5. Read the comprehensive 6-section breakdown:
   - **Hook Analysis**: How they grabbed attention
   - **Content Strategy**: Structure and pacing
   - **Emotional Triggers**: What made viewers feel
   - **Viral Elements**: Specific tactics that worked
   - **Script Techniques**: Writing style and delivery
   - **Replicability**: How you can use these insights

**What the AI analyzes:**
- Video transcript (automatically extracted)
- Title and thumbnail strategy
- Content structure and pacing
- Emotional appeal and storytelling
- Viral frameworks used
- Audience retention patterns

**Use this analysis to:**
- Understand why content performs well
- Identify patterns across successful videos
- Learn proven viral frameworks
- Generate better scripts

## 5. Generate Your First Script

Turn viral insights into your own custom scripts.

**Steps:**
1. From any analyzed video, click "Generate Script"
2. **Select Source Video**: Choose the video to base your script on
3. **Choose Hook Format**: Pick from 9 proven formats:
   - Question Hook: Start with an intriguing question
   - Bold Statement: Make a shocking claim
   - Personal Story: Share a relatable experience
   - Problem/Solution: Address a pain point
   - Curiosity Gap: Create mystery to drive clicks
   - Numbers/Stats: Lead with data
   - Before/After: Show transformation
   - Controversy: Take a strong stance
   - Direct Address: Speak directly to viewer
4. **Select Framework**: Choose your script structure:
   - AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)
   - PAS (Problem, Agitate, Solution)
   - BAB (Before, After, Bridge)
   - PASTOR (Problem, Amplify, Story, Transformation, Offer, Response)
   - 4Ps (Problem, Promise, Proof, Proposal)
   - The Hero's Journey (3-act structure)
   - The Hook-Story-Offer (HSO)
5. **Customize Style** (optional):
   - Set tone: Casual, Professional, Enthusiastic, Educational
   - Adjust length: 30s, 60s, 90s
   - Add personal touches
6. Click "Generate Script"
7. Wait 15-30 seconds for AI to create your script
8. Review your custom script with:
   - Full script text
   - Hook (first 3 seconds)
   - Body (main content)
   - CTA (call-to-action)
   - Timing notes
   - B-roll suggestions

**After generation:**
- Copy the entire script or individual sections
- Download as TXT or PDF
- Favorite for later reference
- Generate variations with different hooks/frameworks

**Free Plan Limits:**
- 20 scripts per month
- All hook formats and frameworks included
- Full customization options

## 6. Organize with Watchlists

Group channels by topic, niche, or inspiration level.

**Steps:**
1. Click "Watchlists" in the sidebar
2. Click "Create Watchlist"
3. Name your watchlist (e.g., "Fitness Creators", "Comedy Inspiration")
4. Add a description (optional)
5. Click "Create"
6. Add channels to your watchlist:
   - From Channels page: Click "Add to Watchlist" on any channel card
   - From Watchlist detail: Click "Add Channels" and select multiple

**Ideas for Watchlists:**
- **By Niche**: Fitness, Finance, Comedy, Education, etc.
- **By Style**: Storytelling, Educational, Entertainment, etc.
- **By Performance**: Top Performers, Consistent, Trending, etc.
- **By Stage**: Study Now, Inspiration, Aspirational, etc.

**Benefits:**
- Quick filtering of videos by topic
- Track specific niches separately
- Monitor competitor performance
- Organize content inspiration

## Common Questions

**Q: How often should I check for new outliers?**
A: Daily or every other day is ideal. ReatorAI syncs channels automatically, so new outliers appear as they happen.

**Q: Can I edit the generated scripts?**
A: Absolutely! Scripts are starting points. Customize them to match your voice, add personal stories, and make them your own.

**Q: What if a video has no transcript?**
A: Some videos don't have captions. Try another video or contact the creator to add captions to their content.

**Q: How do I upgrade my plan?**
A: Click on your profile menu and select "Upgrade" to see plan options.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes! You can download scripts individually or export your entire library from Settings.
